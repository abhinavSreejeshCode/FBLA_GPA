If you have any questions about our gpa caluclator or any questions about gpa, we hope that you will find you answers here

How do I enable the weighted Option?

To enable the weighted option, navigate your way to the add course button, from there, if you look above the button you will find yourself the weighted option

How to do I check my previous GPA

To check you previous gpa to go the top right of your screen. There, you will find a Previous GPA tab where you Previous GPA is located

What is gpa?

GPA stands for grade point average. It is a measure of your grades which colleges and many other people use to examine your academic performance

How do I create an account?

To create an account first go to the top right of your screen. There you should find a Sign up tab. Once you click on it, it will take you to a create account page where you can create an account
